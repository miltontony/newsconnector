The main idea behind News Connector is to relate news articles by their keywords while giving the user a brief description of the article and a link to the full story.

The content is sourced in real time directly from various news websites and is always up to date.

How to

  • Click on the keyword to highlight the related stories.
  • You can also click on the story to see its keywords.
  • Drag the stories and keywords to position them.
  • Ctrl + Click to select multiple stories and keywords.

Toolbar

  • The toolbar allows you to load content from different categores.
  • Clicking on Sports news for example, will load sports news from various news sites.
